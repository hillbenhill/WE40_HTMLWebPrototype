Learning Outcome: ● Produce a digital prototype that demonstrate a user­centric approach toward design and development ● Demonstrate research and data­gathering capabilities by creating personas, scenarios and use cases ● Produce a complete set of UX design documentation including functional and design specifications, wireframes and application flow charts.
