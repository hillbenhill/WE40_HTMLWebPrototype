HAVEN

Smart Home Care For Loved Ones

Haven Concept

Haven is aimed at family, carers and medical professionals of the elderly or disabled people who wish to live in their home safely and independently for as long as possible.

By utilising assistive domotics such as wearable tech and sensors, Haven will record an individual's body readings and track their activities inside and outside the house. The carers will be able to unobtrusively follow a person’s daily routine by reading simple time line charts accessible via website or downloadable applications for tablets and smartphones. Currently we believe there are a lack of appropriate systems which avail of smart home technology to monitor and care for the vulnerable who wish to remain in their home.

The Value Proposition

Haven is a homecare system that uses “home automation” including wearable smart technology sensors. Haven helps family, carers and medical professionals of the elderly or disabled people reduce costs and emotional stress associated with the burden of overall care. Additionally, Haven also allows the common wish of the care recipient to live in their home safely and independently for as long as possible.

With the rapid population ageing that is occurring world-wide, there is increasing interest in “smart home” technologies that can assist older adults to continue living at home with safety and independence.
Empowering the elderly to live independently instead of in an adult care facility not only provides financial savings, it also has the potential to improve quality of life, reduce the strain on aged care facilities, and provide relief for family caregivers.

By utilising assistive domotics (home automation) such as wearable tech and sensors, Haven will record a care recipient’s body readings and track their activities inside and outside the house.
The carers will be able to unobtrusively follow a person’s daily routine by reading simple time line charts accessible via website or downloadable applications for tablets and smartphones.
In addition, Haven’s sensors will be multifunctional, with the ability to act as a traditional security system, as well as monitoring the environment for smoke, gas and Co2.

Following the completion of a Competitor Analysis we believe there are a lack of appropriate systems in the marketplace which avail of smart home technology to monitor and care for the vulnerable who wish to remain in their home.
